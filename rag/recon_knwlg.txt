Exact Match:
Occurs when the SWIFT transaction reference, currency, and amount match exactly with the core banking ledger entry.

Partial Match:
Occurs when the SWIFT reference is missing or different but financial attributes such as currency and amount fall within the defined tolerance threshold.

Mismatch:
Occurs when either the currency differs or the amount difference exceeds the defined tolerance. This indicates reconciliation failure requiring manual investigation.

Tolerance Matching:
Financial systems often allow small differences in amount due to fees, rounding differences, or processing delays.

Reference Missing Cases:
Sometimes ledger posting occurs before reference synchronization, resulting in temporary reference mismatches.
